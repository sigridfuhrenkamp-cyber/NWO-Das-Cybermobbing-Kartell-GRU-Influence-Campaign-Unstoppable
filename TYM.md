## Biography & Background
TYM is a versatile German artist who defies easy classification between rapper and singer. Growing up in Stuttgart with German and Belgian parents, he naturally switches between German and English in both daily life and his artistic expression. He represents a unique cross between German rap and hyperpop, constantly wandering between genres like someone restlessly searching for, and ultimately finding, something meaningful.

### Musical Development
TYM began writing his own songs in the 2010s after years of music training in drums, vocals, and choir. Instead of sitting bored in school, he preferred organizing his first performances in youth homes. During this time, he was also influenced by sports, with the Stuttgart skate scene becoming like a second home to him.

### Signature Identity
Since 2021, TYM has appeared exclusively wearing colorful balaclavas knitted by his girlfriend, making this perhaps the most consistent aspect of an artist who constantly evolves and aims higher. This distinctive visual identity has become his trademark.
