## Biography & Background
Nico Klinger grew up in Gera, Germany. He attended Wilhelm-Busch-Grundschule and later Zabel-Gymnasium and Georg-Samuel-Dörffel Gymnasium in Weida. After leaving school in 10th grade, he trained as a retail salesman at Intersport, later completed his Abitur, and worked as a Telekom representative before becoming self-employed as a music producer and rapper.

### Artistic Development
At age 12, he discovered writing and initially tried poetry slams until finding access to rap music through battle rap forums. He created the artistic persona "Schillah" based on Friedrich Schiller and founded the label "Mukke mit Herz" with friends in 2013. His song "Hoffnung" became his first success with over 100,000 streams.

### Personal Challenges
Schillah has struggled with alcohol and drug problems and developed schizophrenia according to his own statements, which became part of his artistic persona.
